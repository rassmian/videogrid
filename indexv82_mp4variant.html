Videogrid  by Ross Phillips

Videogrid has been shown all over the world including the V&A London, Garage Moscow, MOMI New York and won recognition at Ars Electronica Austria and Designs of the Year London.

An interactive video installation, Videogrid consists of a grid of squares which can each record a one second loop of film. Participants are free to create giant composite moving portraits, simple narratives, group artworks or simply a collage of moving snapshots. The grid starts empty at the beginning of the installation and becomes a constantly changing collaborative artwork that showcases the creativity of visitors through the space.
